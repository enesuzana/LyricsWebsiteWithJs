About "Dance Monkey

Description:

“Dance Monkey” is a song by Australian singer Tones and I, released on 10 May 2019 as the second single from Tones and I’s debut EP The Kids Are Coming. Upon release, Tones and I said the song is about the expectations that are placed on musical performers.
