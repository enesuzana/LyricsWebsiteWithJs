~ About us ~

Our story

Just over a year ago, we became fascinated by the idea of discovering the world through lyrics. What if you could see through the mind of a composer? Or watch the detailed explanation of a meaningful piece of art? While there are many way to discover these, we realized there is no better way to experience a feeling right now than through lyrics. A picture may be worth a thousand words, but a lyric tells the story behind them.
